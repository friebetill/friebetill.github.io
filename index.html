Me?

Let's get to know me

Software Engineer | Founder | Physicist

My name is Till and I love creating things to make life better. I am a software engineer and a founder based in Berlin.

In 2019 I developed TubeCards, a Spaced Repetition cross-platform app with 4.6 rating (over 500 reviews, more than 100k downloads). The app is available on all major platforms and allows users to learn flashcards while being supported by an algorithm deciding on the review dates for maximum learning efficiency.

My interest in software development started at the age of five when I played my first Nintendo game. At the age of 12 I started programming my own websites and at 15 I attended a high school for computer science. Since then, I have completed my Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering at the Technical University of Berlin.

  • Current company: TubeCards
  • City: Berlin, Germany
  • Degree: B. SC. in Computer Engineering
  • Freelance: Available

Besides programming, I love spending time with friends, doing sports and reading books. I also enjoy learning new things and reading stories from the LessWrong community.
