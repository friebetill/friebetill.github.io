TubeCards

Project information

  • Category: App development
  • Client: Personal / Owner
  • Project date: Aug 2018 – Oct 2022
  • Project URL: https://tubecards.app
  • Source code: https://github.com/friebetill/tubecards

The world’s best flashcard platform that is seamlessly integrated with Youtube. TubeCards helps you learn from your favorite YouTubers by providing high-quality flashcards with just one click, bringing you science-based Spaced Repetition to retain knowledge.

I designed, developed and maintain the app so that I could learn French and physics with my fellow students. At that time, there was no flashcards app with which one could easily edit and learn flashcards together. Later, only the YouTube aspect has developed into this.
